# Ghostbusters% (1.4.3.2+ NMG)

Goal: Banish all the overworld ghosts (Revek + Glade of Hope, Cloth, Vespa, Gravedigger, Orthops, Marissa, Poggy Thorax, Joni).

These notes (and splits on HKSM) are for Queen's Station route, as opposed to Greenpath Stag route. 
According to rough timings, the routes are similar in speed, with Queen's Station route possibly being slightly faster.
Queen's Station route doesn't do slopeball to Cliffs, and does Any% Shade Skip as opposed to TE shade skip, and has slightly nicer early game geo.
The routing for Greenpath Stag route will be noted after main notes.


## Vengeful Spirit
- Get the well geo rock [22]
- Aspid arena [~32]
- One of the goam room rocks + husk/vengefly. Right is faster, left can be easier [50]
- Buy Crossroads stag [0]
- Skip FK
- Vengeful Spirit

## Enter Greenpath
- Mound + Greenpath baldurs [90]

## Mothwing Cloak
- Go through Greenpath
- Skip Vengefly King
- Kill Moss Knights + Geo Rocks [~290+]
- Kill Hornet, pick up Mothwing Cloak

## Mantis Claw
- S&Q to Ancestral Mound
- Go to Fungal Wastes
- Do E-Pogo
- Pickup Mantis Claw

## Blue Lake
-Go up from Mantis Village to Queen's Station. You should have at least 290 geo when you get there.
If you are short on geo, you can get the geo rock below the shortcut in fungal.
-Buy Queen's Station [170]
-Stag to Crossroads Stag (1 Up)
-Kill Gruz Mom [220]
-Bench at Salubra
-Buy Shaman's Stone [0]
-Shade skip to Blue Lake (Don't need Sly)
  
## Resting Grounds Stag
- Go through Blue Lake
- Dream platforms to collect Dream Nail
- Seer Skip
- Unlock Resting Grounds Stag

## Deepnest Spa Bench
- Stag to Queen's Station (1 Up)
- Enter Deepnest through the entrance by Spore Shroom
- Bench at the hotspring.

## Galien Essence
- Go left, through the dark room up to Galien.
- If you are worried about the fight, you can grab the lifeblood above Galien for safety
- Kill Galien, collect essence

## Tram Pass
- Go up, get Tram Pass

## Save Cloth
- S&Q to the hotspring
- Go right to tram, call the tram
- Bench in tram, tram right
- Go to the left of the room outside Basin tram
- Save Cloth

## Simple Key
- S&Q as soon as the ground starts shaking (when it splits)
- Go down towards basin simple key
- Slopeball to get over the gap (Remember to set your fps to 50 for consistent slopeball)
- Get the simple key below mawlurks

## Enter Hive Knight
- S&Q to tram
- Tram to the right
- Do wingless hive to Hive Knight

## Dream Nail Vespa
- Kill Hive Knight
- Re-enter the room from the left
- Dream nail Vespa

## Enter Hornet 2
- S&Q to tram
- Climb up from tram to Kingdom's Edge
- Get to Hornet 2 entrance
- Skip up using a primal aspid
- (Safety) Bench before entering Hornet 2
- Enter Hornet 2

## King's Brand
- Kill Hornet 2
- Pick up King's Brand

## Dream Nail Poggy
- Dash out of King's Brand
- Get saved by Hornet (What a twist!)
- Go left, go to the top left transition in the acid room
- Fall down, go to King's Station
- (Safety) Can bench at King's Station
- Go top entrance to the big city room
- Kill Gorgeous Husk [300+]
- Open Pleasure house
- Ride the elevator up, send it down once you reach the top
- Dream nail Marissa
- Go back, call the elevator up, fall down  and dream nail Poggy

## Abyss Door
- Leave Pleasure House
- Go down to basin through broken elevator
- (Safety) Call the tram and bench
- Open Abyss door

## Shade Cloak
- Go down to Abyss (drop towards right side)
- Flick the lighthouse lever
- Get Shade Cloak

## Hidden Station Stag
- S&Q to skip shade cloak animation
- Shade Cloak early control
- Leave abyss
- Slopeball to Hidden Station (Set your fps to 50)
- Unlock Hidden Station Stag [Geo is irrelevant now]

## Spirit Glade Door
- Stag to Resting grounds (1 Up)
- Bench
- Talk to seer until you unlock Spirit Glade

## Glade Cleared
- Go into glade, and dream nail all ghosts (except revek)

## Dream Nail Gravedigger
- S&Q after dream nailing, go back up and dream nail Revek
- S&Q, stag to Queen's Station (1 Up), Bench
- Stag to Dirthmouth (2 Up)
- Dream nail Gravedigger

## Dream Nail Joni
- Go to Howling Cliffs through King's Pass
- Do Joni's Darkroom
- Pick up the charm, dash left, dash right, dream nail Joni

## Enter Queen's Gardens
- S&Q to Queen's Station, climb up to the shade gate QG entrance
- Enter Queen's Gardens

## Dream Nail Caelif and Fera
- Do Petra Arena
- Go to the top right transition
- Dream nail Caelif and Fera

## QG Arena Exit
- (Safety) Bench near Cornifer
- Go down, flick the lever below petra arena
- Go through Queen's Gardens to the Hard Arena, beat it
- Go up through the arena 

## Dream Nai Cloth
- Go up to Traitor Lord
- (Safety) Flick the lever to unlock the shortcut (loses minimal if any time)
- Kill Traitor Lord
- Re-enter the room
- Dream nail Cloth
- GG!

## Regarding Greenpath Stag route:

### Early game changes: 
- Get to 310 geo from moss knights
- Buy Greenpath stag, bench there [170]

- After dash, stag to Crossroads (2 Up)
- Buy shaman stone [0], do TE Shade skip

- From Resting grounds stag, stag to crossroads
- Go to claw, then from claw climb up through fungal shortcut to Spore Shroom entrance to deepnest
- The route is the same from here until the Gravedigger split

- After dream nailing revek, stag to greenpath (1 Up), bench
- Stag to Dirthmouth (2 Up)
- Dream nail Gravedigger
- S&Q to Greenpath stag, take off Shaman Stone
- Go to the baldur shell chest, slopeball up to cliffs (Make sure to set your fps to 50)
- Climb up, do Joni's dark room

- After dream nailing Joni, S&Q to Greenpath stag
- Equip Shaman Stone
- Go down from Greenpath stag, and go right to enter Fog Canyon
- Enter Queen's Gardens

The routes are the same from here until the end.
